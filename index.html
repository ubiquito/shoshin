"In the beginner's mind there are many possibilities,
but in the expert's mind there are few." Shunryu Suzuki

This is not for people who need to be told how to practice.
It is for people who already know, and want to go deeper.

These tools do not explain. They do not encourage. They do not measure your progress or celebrate your completion. They offer a frame — and then they get out of the way.

Can you stay present with what is?
