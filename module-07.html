Nothing Special

"I went and I returned. It was nothing special." Enlightenment is not a special state. It is the full presence in whatever is already happening. Eating lunch is one o'clock. Making tea is the practice. What you are about to do is not a special occasion.

The Practice
Begin the practice

Each day this week: notice one completely ordinary moment. Receive it as complete. No commentary.
