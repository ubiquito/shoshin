# 初心 Shoshin

**A twelve-week practice in beginner's mind.**

Twelve tools drawn from the Zen tradition. For those who already understand.

---

## The Programme

| Week | Practice | What it asks of you |
|------|----------|-------------------|
| 1 | Beginner's Mind | Strike a bowl. Listen. Write what is true. |
| 2 | The Swinging Door | Breathe with the circle. 3-7-8 rhythm. |
| 3 | The Body Knows | Arrange yourself. Hold for one minute. |
| 4 | Mind Weeds | Name a pattern. Watch it become soil. |
| 5 | The Bonfire | Write what you carry. Watch it burn. |
| 6 | The Wide Meadow | Sit in silence for three minutes. |
| 7 | Nothing Special | Go do an ordinary thing. Return. Strike the bowl. |
| 8 | Listening Without Echo | Sit with another person. |
| 9 | One River | Watch the water. |
| 10 | From Nothingness | Enter the silence. Wait. |
| 11 | The Dark Empty Sky | Sit. Conditions change. You are the sky. |
| 12 | Continue | Return to beginner's mind. |

## Principles

- **Nothing leaves your device.** No backend, no tracking, no analytics.
- **The tool gets out of the way.** The practice is what remains.
- **Each tool asks something of your body or your time.** Not just reading.
