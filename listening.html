shoshin

The best way to communicate
may be just to sit
without saying anything.

Sit with this person.
Both of you.

Bring someone.
Sit together.
This practice is for both of you.

Listen without preparing a response.
No echo. No response forming.
Just receive what is here.

When you are ready —
speak one true thing.
Or do not speak.
Both are practice.

When you are ready, each of you may write one thing.

Or leave these empty. Silence is also an answer.

Just watch.
That is enough.
